Shimla Risk Guard — Mandatory Pre-Use Notice

Before proceeding, read and acknowledge the following conditions. This screen appears every session and cannot be skipped or remembered.

01 — Nature of Tool

This is a negative-signal screening aid only. It flags proposals that are likely prohibited under Shimla building regulations. It does not confirm compliance, legality, or approval of any proposal.

02 — No Legal Authority

Output from this tool carries no legal weight. The authoritative sources are the Interim Development Plan (IDP) Shimla 2015 (Notification 13-08-2015, HP Gazette p.2989), the Shimla Planning Area Development Plan 2041, and related gazette notifications. Always read the law. Town & Country Planning Shimla ↗

03 — Human Accountability Required

Every check must be logged and signed by a qualified architect or senior professional. This tool does not replace that accountability.

04 — No Data Retained

Nothing entered here is stored, transmitted, or logged by this software. Each session starts completely fresh.

05 — Scope Limitation

This tool covers a limited ruleset (v1.2). FAR rules are not active — FAR varies by building use category and plot band per IDP Shimla 2015 and cannot be applied without a use-category input. Cases outside this tool's scope return ⚫ OUTSIDE TOOL SCOPE — not clearance.
